About the Owner

Lavender Hill Properties owner Scot E. Henry is a Milwaukee native who has been providing apartment housing at fair rents to area residents since 1997. Scot grew up in Brookfield and moved to the city while in college.


Scot has a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems from University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee and works as a network administrator. He is also an active member of the First Unitarian Society in Milwaukee. Whether handling server issues or tenant requests, Scot strives to deliver fast and friendly service.
